Great tech. Great purpose.

We are Ayup (pronounced ‘ey·up’), a digital product studio from Yorkshire working across the UK. Since our inception in 2014, we have used good design, smart thinking and clever tech to help fix health and social problems.

Over the years we have had the privilege of working with some amazing clients including various NHS organisations, local authorities and charities with a mission of using technology as a force for good.

We are now focussing all of our experience, knowledge and skills to concentrate our efforts into one particular problem space where we believe we can have the most impact.
